MIXOLOGY CLOTHING COMPANY

Mixology Clothing Co.'s Story

With a desire to take their family-founded operation from its impressive 2010 roots into today's modern world, Mixology Clothing Co. teamed up with us to create a timeless and infinitely scaleable omnichannel brand identity!

We focused on brand revitalization + extension, digital presence + partnerships, in-store marketing, email strategy, and UI/UX design for web.

Currently, Mixology continues to grow and has expanded to include a variety of programs and collaborations. Mixology’s streamlined emails have more than doubled their online sales and boasts an instagram presence of over 200K followers, which. drives 50% of their online sales.

SANTULAN CREATIVE

Santulan Creative's Story

A testament to our one of a kind values, Santulan Creative is rapidly growing, stand-out agency that puts POC and LGBTQIA+ communities at the forefront.

We began working with Santulan to create a brand identity that was as unique and colorful as their mission and ethics.

After two extremely fruitful (print + digital) magazine launches, we were able to gain a deep understanding of Santulan’s driving forces as well as their diverse network of creatives. We then spent the next three months ensuring that Santulan’s voice reached every last corner of their brand!

Today, Santulan is a fully-fleshed-out brand whose presence speaks for itself. They continue to surpass revenue goals while creating opportunities for communities often overlooked!
