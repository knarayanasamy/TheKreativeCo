THIS IS A HOME


And it's full of love.



As a singular person, I recycle old candle wax to make new art. All proceeds of these candles go to a long list of charities, causes, and groups. You can join in or learn more here.

More importantly, are we spreading enough love? Are we listening? Are we really truly supporting each other? Due to the fact that this website doubles as my portfolio, I find it imperative that I be open and honest about the causes and movements I support. They are a part of my everyday life—a part of my soul. There is no room here for hate; for anti-Black, anti-LGBTQ+, anti-immigrant sentiments.

There only room here is room for love and community.

Don’t like what you’re reading? That’s ok. Thank you for dropping by anyway!
