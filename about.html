ABOUT KN


Hi there! I'm Kerishma Narayanasamy; I'm a self-taught web designer, digital illustrator, photographer, and creator in regard to everything housed within this website. As of now, I'm letting my surroundings and love for music, food, and nature influence my work in all forms.

My design-adjascent passions lean heavily towards branding & being a shapeshifter—emulating brand voices to a tee & being able to use my background in programming to help me navigate all that I do. And, of course, I'd be delighted to work with you!

When I’m not gazing longingly into a computer screen, I bide my time watching 80s movies, jamming to whatever music my friends happen to be making, being a complete goof, and fawning over every animal I lay eyes on. I also sell candles and jewelry to benefit a variety of causes, charities, and groups. You can learn more about that here.
